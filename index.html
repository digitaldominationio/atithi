ATITHI

Real-Time Guest Monitoring System

Powered by Commissionerate Police, Bhubaneswar-Cuttack, Odisha

Atithi is an advanced portal that enables hotels to register guests while providing law enforcement with real-time monitoring capabilities. Instantly detect and receive alerts when suspects or persons of interest check into registered hotels, ensuring public safety through seamless collaboration.

Key Features

Real-Time Monitoring

Instant detection and alerts when suspects check into hotels, enabling swift law enforcement response.

Instant Notifications

Automated popup alerts notify authorities immediately when a match is found in the police database.

Database Integration

Seamlessly connected with the police database for accurate and up-to-date suspect information.

Analytics & Reports

Comprehensive reports and analytics to track guest patterns and suspicious activities.

Secure Access

Role-based access control ensuring data security and authorized personnel access only.

Multi-Hotel Network

Connect multiple hotels across Bhubaneswar and Cuttack for comprehensive coverage.

About Atithi

A collaborative initiative between the hotel industry and Commissionerate Police to ensure public safety through technology-driven guest monitoring.
